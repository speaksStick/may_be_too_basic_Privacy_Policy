Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-14

Introduction
May Be Too Basic ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: account information, profile, support requests.
  • Usage information: anonymized analytics about app performance and feature usage.
  • Device information: OS, app version, device identifiers for diagnostics.
  • Optional data for features: any habit data you create in the App is stored locally and (if applicable) in services you enable.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the App's functionality.
  • Improve the App (analytics, diagnostics).
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Local Storage & Sync

Habit data is stored locally using Hive. If you enable cloud sync or backup features, that data may be transferred to the configured backend. Review feature-level settings before enabling syncing.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, and billing. These services have their own privacy terms. We do not sell personal data.

AI Features

AI insights are available only if enabled in the app. For closed-testing builds these features are disabled; if enabled, prompts and minimal anonymized context may be sent to the AI provider. Do not include highly sensitive data in prompts.

Billing & Purchases

Billing is handled by the platform (Google Play / App Store). We do not handle credit card information directly. Restore and purchase receipts may be validated via platform APIs.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data where applicable. Contact us at the address below.

Children

The App is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under applicable minimum ages.

Data Retention & Security

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the App. We use industry-standard measures to protect data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact: speaksstick@gmail.com

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change. Major changes will be communicated via release notes.
